Privacy Policy

Analyst Prep Kit · Last updated July 5, 2026

Plain-language summary, provided for transparency. It is not legal advice; please review with a professional before relying on it.

The short version

You can use the Analyst Prep Kit with no account — your learning progress is saved only in your own browser and never leaves your device. Signing in is optional: if you choose to create an account (with an email and password, or with Google), we store your email and sync your progress to our backend so it follows you across devices. We use privacy-respecting analytics to understand general usage.

What we collect

Anonymous usage analytics. We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to see aggregate, anonymized information — pages visited, general location (country/region), and device type — so we can improve the kits. We do not use this to identify you personally.

Your progress (stored on your device). Lesson progress, settings, and any premium unlock are stored in your browser's local storage. If you don't sign in, this stays on your device — we never receive or store it — and clearing your browser data or switching devices removes it.

Account & sync data (only if you sign in). Creating an account is optional and only needed to sync your progress across devices. If you do, we store your email address (and, for Google sign-in, the basic profile Google provides) along with a synced copy of your learning progress and any premium entitlement. This is held in our backend, hosted by Supabase, and is used only to restore your progress and unlocks when you sign in on another device. The entire free kit works without an account.

Payment information (only if you buy). If you purchase the All-Access Pass, the transaction is handled entirely by Buy Me a Coffee and its payment processor, Stripe. We never see or store your card details. They may collect your email and billing details under their own privacy policies.

Cookies

Google Analytics sets cookies to measure usage. You can block these with your browser settings or a browser extension such as the Google Analytics Opt-out add-on. The kits work fully without analytics cookies.

What we don't do

We don't sell your data, run advertising networks, build personal profiles, or share data except with the analytics, authentication/sync, and payment providers named above strictly to operate the service.

Your choices

You can clear your local progress any time from within each kit's settings, opt out of analytics as described above, and — if you created an account — sign out on any device or email us to delete your account and the progress synced to it. You can also ask what (if anything) is associated with a purchase.
