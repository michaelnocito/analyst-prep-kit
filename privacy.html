Privacy Policy

Analyst Prep Kit · Last updated June 26, 2026

Plain-language summary, provided for transparency. It is not legal advice; please review with a professional before relying on it.

The short version

The Analyst Prep Kit has no accounts, no logins, and no passwords. We don't ask for your name or email to use the kits. We use privacy-respecting analytics to understand general usage, and your learning progress is saved only in your own browser.

What we collect

Anonymous usage analytics. We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to see aggregate, anonymized information — pages visited, general location (country/region), and device type — so we can improve the kits. We do not use this to identify you personally.

Your progress (stored on your device). Lesson progress, settings, and any premium unlock are stored in your browser's local storage. This stays on your device — we never receive or store it. Clearing your browser data or switching devices removes it.

Payment information (only if you buy). If you purchase the All-Access Pass, the transaction is handled entirely by Buy Me a Coffee and its payment processor, Stripe. We never see or store your card details. They may collect your email and billing details under their own privacy policies.

Cookies

Google Analytics sets cookies to measure usage. You can block these with your browser settings or a browser extension such as the Google Analytics Opt-out add-on. The kits work fully without analytics cookies.

What we don't do

We don't sell your data, run advertising networks, build personal profiles, or share data except with the analytics and payment providers named above strictly to operate the service.

Your choices

You can clear your local progress any time from within each kit's settings, opt out of analytics as described above, and contact us to ask what (if anything) is associated with a purchase.
